03 · Insider Intel

Fan Tips for London

Quick plays from the locals' playbook
🚇

Ride the Tube

Tap your US contactless card — no Oyster needed. Avoid the Central line during rush hour. Mind the gap. It's literal.

🍺

Pub Etiquette

Order at the bar, not the table. No tipping expected. Last orders bell rings 15 mins before closing. Don't miss a proper pint of bitter.

🚕

Black Cab Culture

London cabbies pass "The Knowledge" — a 3-year exam. They're your best source of local intel. Always tip them, they've earned it.

⏰

Book Ahead

London's best experiences sell out fast, especially during NFL season. Book your tours the moment you land — you won't regret it.

🇺🇸

Americans in London

Benjamin Franklin lived here. John Adams too. London shaped the very ideas of your nation — our Founding Fathers tour tells the whole story.

📸

Photo Hotspots

Tower Bridge at dusk. Parliament from Westminster Bridge. Greenwich Hill view. The glass walkway at One New Change. All free. All extraordinary.
